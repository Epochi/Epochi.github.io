Amadea

Development Stack: Jekyll, Bootstrap

Design

Client required a simple website that lists all their services with prices and descriptions. There was a bit of a challenge when thinking about how to structure the content. While researching websites from the same category it seemed like most chose to list their services like an e-shop and the resulting websites felt overwhelming because of the huge amounts of information and difficulty moving from one service and its description to other.

My main goal was ease of use and I decided to use nested tabs. First, together with the client we restructured the services into branching categories to display the information in smaller chunks. It shows the user only relevant information and by having the descriptions in a tab next to prices, user can easily navigate around without losing their track in the list.
